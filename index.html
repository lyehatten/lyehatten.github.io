about me.

I'm levi hatten, a student at swarthmore college studying music, media, and culture. due to the covid-19 crisis I decided to take a leave of absence from swarthmore and spend some time working on myself and my skills.
this lead my to eleven fifty academy where I have been expanding my coding knowledge.
in high school I had a passion for html and css. I would edit blog themes and add as many hover effects as possible, but when I explored computer science classes in high school it wasn't the same.
working with c++ and java limited my creative aspirations and I found myself throwing away my plans to become a computer science major.

once I matriculated at swarthmore I tried out a variety of classes and settled on combining film and media studies and music. this has helped me explore my passions for music and film. as a musician, I am constantly inspired by the visual arts and have a deep appreciation for film.
